Draft a first-contact letter to a customer whose loan payment is 30 days past
due. Requirements:

- Tone: respectful and supportive, never threatening. Assume the missed
  payment may be an oversight or temporary hardship.
- Clearly state the account is past due and the amount owed placeholder:
  [AMOUNT], due date placeholder: [DUE DATE].
- Offer the standard contact channels: phone line [PHONE] and secure message.
- Mention that hardship assistance options exist, without describing
  specific programs or eligibility.
- Do not mention credit reporting consequences in this first letter.
- Keep it under 250 words, at an 8th-grade reading level.
